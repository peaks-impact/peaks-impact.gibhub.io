Find Us in Zurich

PEAKS is located in Zurich, Switzerland's largest city and a global hub for finance, technology, and research. Our workspace sits at the heart of the city, with easy access to Zurich's ecosystem as well as a wide variety of food options.

Address

Zeughausstrasse 31

8004 Zurich, Switzerland

Easy access via public transportation from Zurich HB and Tram stations Sihlpost/HB and Stauffacher

Close to ETH Zurich and University of Zurich

Upcoming Events

PEAKS regularly hosts events in collaboration with Zurich AI Safety, EA Zurich and the broader Swiss EA community. From relaxed gatherings of like-minded people to lightning talks and discussions, our events provide opportunities to learn and connect with the community.

Our Community

PEAKS hosts a diverse and talented community of researchers, professionals, and organizations working at the intersection of Effective Altruism and AI safety. Our members are united by a shared commitment to using evidence and reason to tackle the world's most pressing problems.

From established organizations to independent researchers, our community spans multiple disciplines and approaches, creating a rich environment for collaboration and knowledge exchange.
